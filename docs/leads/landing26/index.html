Description

Contemporary 5-bed villa (2020) in Huerta Belón, just a 10-minute walk to Marbella Old Town and the beach. Bright open-plan living flows to generous terraces and a landscaped garden with heated pool and jacuzzi. Master suite with balcony, UFH in bathrooms and fitted wardrobes. Cinema room, gym, laundry and storage. Solar panels, AC, security system and secure on-plot parking. A turnkey luxury home steps from restaurants, shops and schools; 10 min drive to Puerto Banús.
Stylish villa marrying convenience and privacy in central Marbella. Sun-filled open-plan lounge/diner connects via large sliders to terraces for alfresco dining amid lush greenery. Five spacious bedrooms, including an impressive primary with private balcony and luxe en-suite; all with AC and fitted storage. Wellness and entertainment include a private cinema and a fully equipped gym. Outdoors: heated pool, jacuzzi and landscaped garden. Extras: solar panels, modern security, laundry, built-in storage and secure parking.
Prime central location balancing tranquillity and access. A flat 10-minute walk reaches Marbella’s Old Town and Playa de la Fontanilla. Puerto Banús is about 10 minutes by car, and La Cañada shopping mall is roughly 5 minutes away. Daily needs are close by, with schools, cafés, supermarkets and a tennis & padel club within a short walk. Quick road access to A-7/AP-7 for easy links along the Costa del Sol.
